What is DSIP MapManager?

DSIP MapManager is a free online geographic asset management tool built on Google Maps. It helps municipalities, utility companies, field teams, and infrastructure managers track, organize, and maintain physical assets across large geographic areas — directly from the browser.

Whether you need to map fire hydrants across a city, inventory road signs along highways, or track street lights, utility poles, and other infrastructure — MapManager gives you an interactive map interface with powerful tools to manage it all.

Map Utilities & Key Features

map

Interactive Google Maps

Full Google Maps integration with satellite, terrain, and street view. Geocoding search to find any address worldwide. Smooth zooming and panning with marker clustering for large datasets.

push_pin

Custom Geo Markers

Create custom marker types with your own SVG icons. Over 80 built-in icons for fire hydrants, road signs, and default markers. Each marker stores title, status, condition, priority, cost, dates, and notes.

folder_shared

Project-Based Organization

Organize assets into separate projects — one for hydrants, another for road signs, another for street furniture. Each project has its own map center, zoom level, and team members.

groups

Team Collaboration

Invite team members by email with Admin or Viewer roles. Admins manage markers, Viewers have read-only access. Role-based access control enforced at both UI and API level for security.

build

Maintenance & Cost Tracking

Track installation dates, warranty expiry, maintenance schedules, repair history, and costs for every asset. Assign responsible persons and set priority levels. Monitor overall infrastructure spending.

Photo Documentation

Attach photos to any marker for field documentation. Upload images directly from your phone during inspections. All photos are securely stored per user with access control.

translate

Multi-Language Support

Full interface available in English, Romanian, German, and French. Switch languages instantly from the dashboard. All labels, buttons, and messages are fully translated.

Dark & Light Themes

Choose between dark and light mode for comfortable use in any lighting condition. The map and all interface elements adapt to your preferred theme. Settings are saved per user.

Secure Authentication

Sign in safely with your Google account via OAuth 2.0. No passwords to manage. Whitelist-based access control ensures only authorized users can use the platform.

Who Uses MapManager?

Map every fire hydrant in your municipality. Track hydrant type (above-ground, underground, wall-mounted), status (active, maintenance, inactive), condition, installation date, and last inspection. Assign maintenance to team members and monitor warranty expiry dates. Over 24 specialized hydrant icons included.

Create a complete digital inventory of road signs along any road network. Track 55 different road sign types with dedicated icons — from speed limits and stop signs to directional and warning signs. Record sign condition, reflectivity status, installation date, and replacement schedule.

Manage any type of city infrastructure: street lights, utility poles, park benches, waste bins, bus stops, drainage covers, bridges, public buildings. Custom marker types let you define your own asset categories with custom SVG icons tailored to your specific use case.

Track utility infrastructure across large geographic areas. Plan and manage field inspections, assign maintenance tasks to technicians, document asset conditions with photos, and monitor repair costs. Ideal for water, gas, electric, and telecom utility management.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is DSIP MapManager really free?

Yes. DSIP MapManager is free to use for authorized users. Sign in with your Google account and start managing geographic assets immediately. There are no hidden fees or premium tiers.

What types of assets can I track on the map?

Anything with a geographic location: fire hydrants, road signs, street lights, utility poles, park equipment, waste containers, drainage systems, buildings, and more. You can create custom marker types with your own SVG icons for any asset category.

Can multiple people work on the same project?

Yes. Project administrators can invite team members by email. Each member gets either Admin access (full editing rights) or Viewer access (read-only). All changes are synced in real-time across the team.

Does it work on mobile devices?

Yes. The interface is fully responsive and works on smartphones, tablets, and desktops. You can add markers and upload photos directly from your phone during field inspections.

What data can I store for each marker?

Each marker supports: title, geographic coordinates, icon type, status (active/maintenance/inactive), condition (good/fair/poor/critical), priority level, cost, currency, installation date, maintenance date, repair date, warranty date, responsible person, observations, custom JSON data, and photo attachments.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Authentication is handled through Google OAuth 2.0. The application runs in a Docker container with security hardening: read-only filesystem, dropped capabilities, no-new-privileges flag, and resource limits. Access is controlled via whitelist and role-based permissions.
